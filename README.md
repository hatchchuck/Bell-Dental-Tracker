# Bell-Dental-Tracker

Tracks dental visits across the family (Hannah, Chandler, Kristen, Riley, Carsen) at Bell Dentistry.

Bell is paid out of pocket at time of service. Once Delta Dental reimburses part of that charge, the net cost is known and that's the one amount pulled from the HSA — no repayments, no Form 8889 excess to track.

Data syncs through the shared gist-proxy (same pattern as Hannah-Budget / Chandler's app / Carsen's dashboard).
